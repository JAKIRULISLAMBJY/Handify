Project Overview

In this project, we helped a homeowner transform their modern living room into a cleaner and more functional space by mounting their flat-screen TV and organizing the cable system. The client wanted a clean, elegant setup with no visible wires—balancing both aesthetics and safety. This project is a perfect example of how thoughtful handyman work can enhance both the look and practicality of a home.

Project Goals

Transforming a cluttered entertainment setup into a sleek, modern feature—this project focused on mounting a large flat-screen TV securely to the living room wall while concealing all cables for a clean, minimalist look. We carefully selected a heavy-duty wall bracket suited to the client’s wall type and screen size, ensuring safety and optimal viewing height.

  • Safely mount a 65” flat-screen TV to the wall
  • Optimize viewing angle and height
  • Hide all power and media cables for a clean finish
  • Ensure child safety and reduce clutter
  • Match the modern design aesthetic of the living space

Outcome

The result is a visually clean and organized entertainment space. By hiding the cables and elevating the TV, the room now feels more spacious, modern, and inviting. The client was thrilled with how the sleek setup elevated the look of their home and simplified their everyday media experience.
